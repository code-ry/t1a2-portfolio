A wealth of life experience with a positive attitude

I move forward with an open mind and a hunger to learn. Whatever the challenge I will strive for success.

My story so far...

Growing up in the country town of Wagga Wagga in a military family, I have an expansive imagination combined with a systematic approach to life. I have since travelled to many parts of Australia and the world in the search of new and confronting experiences. Moving out of the family home at the age of 17 to travel New Zealand and go snowboarding, I developed a confidence for my abilities and decision making process to get me where I want to go. Throughout my 20's working in kitchens across the country, I enjoyed working within a team of individuals, striving towards a common goal. Now in my early 30's, I seek to build on all of my previous experiences and expand my knowledge into the tech industry.

What I believe in...

Inclusivity

Welcoming and engaging with people from all walks of life is something I treasure. With differing backgrounds and experiences comes a wealth of knowledge that can not be replicated.

Sustainability

Protecting our planet and in essence our future is a core value of mine. I hope to engage with fellow sustainable entities to better the place we call home.

Progession

While appreciating the past is very valuable, I believe we must use what we have learnt to move forward and be ever evolving. Every challenge is a chance to better myself and those around me.

Family and Community

Spending time and appreciating the ones I love comes of the highest importance to me and is the driving factor to my existance. To share my highs and lows with the ones around me gives my great satisfaction.

Quality of work

The saying "If you are going to do something, do it right" resonates in me. I believe in taking great pride in my work and undertake these tasks with passion.

Enjoying the journey

Taking time to enjoy the ride and smell the roses along the way makes it all worthwhile for me. After all what is the point of a destination if you don't appreciate how you go there.
